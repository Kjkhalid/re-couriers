Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy regulates the practices of REC concerning the gathering, utilization, maintenance, and revelation of data acquired from users (“Users”) of the https://re-courier.com website (“Site”). This policy applies to the Site, as well as to all products and services provided by REC.

Personal Identification Information

We may acquire personal identification information from Users through various means, including but not limited to their visits to our site, placement of orders, completion of forms, and engagement in other activities, services, features, or resources provided on our Site. Such information may encompass, as appropriate, names, email addresses, mailing addresses, and phone numbers. Users do have the option to navigate our Site anonymously. We shall only collect personal identification information from Users if they willingly submit such information to us. However, Users retain the right to decline the provision of personally identifiable information, except when such action may hinder their participation in certain Site-related activities.

Non-Personal Identification Information

Whenever Users engage with our Site, we may gather non-personal identification information, which can encompass browser names, computer types, technical data regarding Users’ connections to our Site (such as operating systems and Internet service providers), and comparable information.

Web Browser Cookies

Our Site might utilize “cookies” to enhance User experience. These cookies are placed on Users’ hard drives by their web browsers for record-keeping purposes and at times for tracking purposes. Users have the prerogative to configure their web browsers to either refuse cookies or to alert them when cookies are being transmitted. In the event of such adjustments, it is worth noting that certain aspects of the Site may not function optimally.

Use of Collected Information

R Courier may gather and employ Users’ personal information for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Enhancing Customer Service: The information you furnish aids us in responding to your customer service queries and support needs in a more efficient manner.
  • Personalizing User Experience: We might employ aggregated information to comprehend how our Users collectively engage with the services and resources offered on our Site.
  • Site Enhancement: Your feedback might be employed to augment our products and services.
  • Dispatch of Periodic Emails: We may use your email address to convey User information and updates related to their orders. Additionally, it could be utilized to address their inquiries, queries, and other requests.

Protection of Information

We have implemented appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices, alongside security measures, to counteract unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information, usernames, passwords, transaction information, and data stored on our Site. The exchange of sensitive and private data between the Site and Users occurs over a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) secured communication channel, and such data is encrypted and protected by digital signatures.

Personal Information Sharing

Users’ personal identification information is not sold, traded, or leased to others. We may share aggregated demographic information that is devoid of personal identification details with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers for the purposes stated above.

Amendments to this Privacy Policy

Rush Expedited Courier holds the discretion to modify this privacy policy as deemed necessary. In such cases, the updated date will be reflected at the bottom of this page. We urge Users to regularly consult this page for any alterations, in order to remain informed about how we safeguard the personal information we gather. Users acknowledge and consent that it is their responsibility to periodically review this privacy policy and stay informed about any changes.

Acceptance of Terms

Through the utilization of this Site, you express your acceptance of this policy and the accompanying terms of service. Should you dissent with this policy, we kindly request that you refrain from using our Site. Continued utilization of the Site subsequent to the posting of modifications to this policy signifies your acceptance of such changes.
