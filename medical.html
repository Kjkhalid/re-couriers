Medical Courier Services

At Rush Expedited Courier, we understand the unique demands of healthcare logistics and are dedicated to providing trustworthy, compliant, and timely courier services tailored specifically for the medical industry. From hospitals and clinics to laboratories and pharmaceutical companies, we deliver critical medical supplies with the precision and care they require.

We adhere to stringent regulatory standards, including HIPAA (USA), PIPEDA (Canada), OSHA, and DOT guidelines, ensuring your deliveries are fully compliant with both Canadian and U.S. regulations. Whether you’re shipping pharmaceuticals, medical specimens, or equipment, you can trust Rush Expedited Courier to handle your sensitive materials efficiently and securely.

Why Choose Rush Expedited Courier for Your Medical Deliveries?

Specialized Handling of Medical Goods

Our couriers are trained to handle medical items—such as pharmaceuticals, biological specimens, and sensitive equipment—with strict protocols, ensuring safety and compliance throughout the delivery process.

Comprehensive Coverage

With our extensive network, we provide reliable delivery across North America, both locally and nationwide. Whether delivering urgent lab samples or medical equipment, we have the infrastructure to meet your needs.

Temperature-Controlled Solutions

For temperature-sensitive items like vaccines, blood samples, and pharmaceuticals, we offer advanced climate-controlled vehicles to ensure proper handling and delivery within required conditions.

Real-Time Tracking

We offer real-time tracking so you can monitor your deliveries from start to finish, ensuring transparency and confidence in our service.

Regulatory Compliance

We strictly follow all regulatory standards, including HIPAA and PIPEDA, ensuring the confidentiality and security of sensitive healthcare materials. Our team is well-versed in these regulations, giving you peace of mind that your shipments are safe and compliant.
